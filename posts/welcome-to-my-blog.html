Why Start a Blog?

In an age of social media and instant messaging, you might wonder why anyone would start a traditional blog. For me, it's about having a space that's truly mineâ€”a digital garden where I can plant ideas and watch them grow.

Here are a few reasons why I decided to start this blog:

  • Long-form thinking: Social media encourages quick takes and hot opinions. A blog allows for deeper, more thoughtful exploration of topics.
  • Ownership: This is my space, not rented from a platform that could change its rules at any moment.
  • Learning in public: Writing helps me clarify my thinking and learn from the feedback of others.
  • Connection: Blogs create opportunities to connect with like-minded people around the world.
